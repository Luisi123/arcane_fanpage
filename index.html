Welcome to the Arcane show

Arcane is a visually stunning animated series set in the universe of League of Legends, exploring the conflict between the utopian city of Piltover and the oppressed underground of Zaun. It follows the tragic story of two sisters, Vi and Jinx, whose bond is shattered by war, political intrigue, and the rise of dangerous technology. With breathtaking animation, deep character development, and an emotional narrative, Arcane has been widely praised as one of the best video game adaptations ever made.
